# ABMLP-XE

*ABMLP-Extended-(X) and Enhanced(E)*

ABMLP-XE supersedes ABMLP-X's conventional gilt-based framework. The operational flow and logic of ABMLP-XE is a purely conceptual exercise \- not expected to ever be a highly simplified representation of a real-world UK monetary system. Nevertheless, Its development would still hold value and interest.
